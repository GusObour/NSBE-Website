National Society of Black Engineers | Region 5

Our Mission Statement

To increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community.

Academic Support

Collect the necessary expertise to exceed in challenging curriculum. Obtain study skills and feedback from mentors, workshops, study groups, and more.

Professional Development

Attend workshops designed to help attendees attain and negotiate offers, communicate with confidence, and outperform expectations in any professional situation. Utilize knowledge from regional and national conferences.

Community Engagement

Encourage higher education and STEM exploration within local communities through direct engagement. Leverage technical and communication skills to make an impact on curious learners of all ages.
